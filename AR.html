Augumented Reality

General explanation

Augumented Reality means you add virtual objects in the world, the best example for this is the popular game "Pokemon Go".

More examples:

  • You can use it for navigation
  • For building instructions
  • To help you choose the right furniture
  • For education with the Deep Frame Display
  • To help firefighters or the ambulance see dangers, fire sources or persons in need of help

    • Technology

    Most of the AR-Headsets use Inside-Out-Tracking, that means they don´t need external tracking sensor like the HTC Vive (Pro) or the Oculus Rift do.

    There are many other Windows MR-Headsets like the Samsung Odyssey from the different brands like Acer, HP, Lenvov or Medion the only difference would be disply or production quality.
