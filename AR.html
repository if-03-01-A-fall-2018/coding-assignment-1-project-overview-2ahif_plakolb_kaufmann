Augumented Reality

General explanation

Augumented Reality means you add virtual objects in the real world, the best examples for this are: the popular game "Pokemon Go", Snapchat filters, Apple Memoji, Samsung AR Emoji

More examples:

You can use it for..

  • navigation
  • building instructions
  • helping you choose the right furniture
  • helping education with the Deep Frame Display
  • helping firefighters or the ambulance see dangers, fire sources or persons in need of help

Technology

Most of the AR-Headsets use Inside-Out-Tracking, that means they don´t need external tracking sensor like the HTC Vive (Pro) or the Oculus Rift do.

There are many other Windows MR-Headsets like the Samsung Odyssey from the different brands like Acer, HP, Lenvov or Medion the only difference would be disply or production quality.
